Onward & Upward.

Welcome to Blue Triangle.

Posted by John Barton on February 1, 2017

This is it.

As a senior in high school, I started Tangible Productions. It started as just a small group of friends hanging out and shooting silly videos after school and on the weekends. Once I got to the University of Florida, I brought Tangible with me, and it grew rapidly from there - beyond my wildest expectations. But this isn't a history lesson. On the contrary, this is a look to the future.

My future lies with Blue Triangle Productions - a company I've been quietly building for the past few months. Blue Triangle is now the public face of Media You Can Feel, LLC. This is more than just re-branding the public and business side of MYCF from Tangible to Blue Triangle. This is a new endeavor entirely. One that I believe in and one that I know can be successful.

While we're here, I want to clear up a few things that might be confusing about this new effort.

"Tangible Productions" still exists as a student organization at the University of Florida, operating (for the most part) independently of MYCF. I'm not involved in their day-to-day operations (nor do I wish to be). My focus lies entirely in Blue Triangle Productions. Currently, Media You Can Feel, LLC, financially supports Tangible Productions as their sole sponsor. Tangible Productions is using a full production setup composed entirely of equipment owned by MYCF. Any internal resources are also owned by MYCF. Tangible's not going away anytime soon, but we wanted to fully separate the LLC and the student organization's YouTube channels, social media accounts, etc., in order to give a more accurate representation of the LLC's production capabilities and professionalism - which is what prompted the creation of Blue Triangle.

Blue Triangle was created with one major goal: to continually get better. Continual and sustained improvement is key in any organization, and Blue Triangle is no exception. Each day, I strive to make Blue Triangle better, even in just some small way. We want to move onward & upward.

I'd be remiss if I claimed to be doing all of this on my own. Other people have always played a significant role in the development of my production company. I'm building a team here that can help bring Blue Triangle (and MYCF, of course) to a whole new level.

This is it. This is the future of Media You Can Feel.

John Barton Founder & President

John Barton, founder of Media You Can Feel, LLC, and Blue Triangle Productions, has been working in video production since 2011.
